About me

Hey there! I'm Jales Nunes, an IT enthusiast turned aspiring web developer and IoT enthusiast. My journey into the world of web development began during my time as a financial advisor in an IT company. Being involved in IT projects and making friends within the IT world sparked my curiosity about coding and technology.

I'm particularly fascinated by the intersection of web development and IoT, envisioning a future where seamless integration between devices and the web enhances our everyday lives. One concept that captivates me is home automation, especially the idea of controlling everything through voice commands.

While I'm still exploring the tools and technologies necessary for implementing voice command functionality, I'm eager to learn more about web libraries like React and frameworks like Next.js. I see these as crucial steps in advancing my skills and expanding my capabilities in both building websites and applications.

Although I haven't yet defined specific projects or goals, I'm excited about the endless possibilities these technologies offer. As I continue on this coding adventure, I hope to leverage my newfound knowledge to create impactful solutions and enriching experiences for users, contributing to the ever-evolving landscape of web development and IoT.

Let's learn and grow together on this exciting journey!
